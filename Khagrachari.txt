                                                            Khagrachari

Khagrachhari is a district in south-eastern Bangladesh. It is a part of the Chittagong Division and the Chittagong Hill Tracts. Its local name is "Chengmi". Khagrachhari is also known as Phalang Htaung or the Mong Circle. Khagrachhari town is the home town of the Mong Chief who is the head of the Arakanese descendants living in the circle.
