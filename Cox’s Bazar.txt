                                                           Cox’s Bazar

Cox's Bazar is a city, fishing port, tourism centre and district headquarters in Bangladesh. The beach in Cox's Bazar is sandy and has a gentle slope; with a unbroken length of 120 km, it is the longest natural sea beach in the world.
