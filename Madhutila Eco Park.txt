                                                          Madhutila Eco Park

Madhutila Eco Park is in Puragaon Union under the Madhutila Forest Range in Nalitabari Upazila of Sherpur district of Bangladesh in 1999. It is 380 acres.[1][3] It falls close to the Bangladesh-India border.
Madhutila Eco Park include region Mymensingh, Sherpur, Jamalpur, Tangail, Netrokona and Kishoreganj districts.
